Bridging Silence: Team Organization Document
Project Overview
"Bridging Silence" is an AI-powered system designed to help individuals who are Deaf or Hard of Hearing communicate with hearing individuals in real time. The system captures video input of sign language, translates it into text, and converts that text into audible speech.
